# Mohamed Salah's Remarkable Goal Contributions: How Liverpool's Egyptian King Compares to Premier League's Elite Scorers

## Breaking Down Salah's Season

What makes Salah's 2025-26 campaign particularly noteworthy is the balance he has maintained. Under Arne Slot's tactical framework, the Egyptian has found improved fluidity in Liverpool's attacking system, allowing him to transition seamlessly between provider and finisher.

Of his 28 goals, Salah has scored from a diverse range of positions:

- **Right-wing finishes**: 14 goals
- **Cutting inside to left foot**: 9 goals
- **Penalty conversions**: 3 goals
- **Headers**: 2 goals

His 12 assists represent his most consistent creative output in recent seasons, benefiting the likes of Darwin Nunez, Cody Gakpo, and Luis Diaz. The Egyptian's partnership with these attacking talents has created a fluidity that mirrors some of his best seasons in a Liverpool shirt.
