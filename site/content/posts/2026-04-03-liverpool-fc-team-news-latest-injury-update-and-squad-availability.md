# Liverpool FC Team News: Slot Assesses Squad Fitness Ahead of Crucial Fixture

Arne Slot has provided an encouraging update on Liverpool FC's injury situation, with several key players expected to feature in the coming weeks as the Reds push for silverware during the 2025-26 season.

The Liverpool manager addressed the media in his latest press conference, revealing that the club's medical team has made significant progress in their rehabilitation programme following the international break. With fixtures coming thick and fast, Slot is carefully managing his squad's workload to ensure maximum availability for Anfield's demanding campaign.

## Key Players Progressing Well

Mohamed Salah continues his recovery from a minor knock sustained in recent weeks, with sources suggesting the Egyptian winger is on track to return to full training next week. Slot confirmed that Salah remains integral to Liverpool's plans and should be available for selection in the coming fixture, though the manager hinted at possible rotation to manage his workload.

Virgil van Dijk, Liverpool's commanding captain, has resumed full training without restrictions. The Dutch defender's leadership on and off the pitch has been crucial to Liverpool's defensive solidity, and Slot expressed confidence in his availability for upcoming matches.

Central defender Ibrahima Konate has also returned to first-team training following a brief absence, providing Slot with additional defensive options alongside van Dijk and Joe Gomez.

## Midfield Reinforcements

In the engine room, Ryan Gravenberch has stepped up his recovery work and is expected to resume team training within the next fortnight. The Dutch midfielder's absence has been felt, with Alexis Mac Allister stepping in admirably to fill the void. Slot praised Mac Allister's professionalism whilst suggesting Gravenberch's return will provide valuable squad depth.

Curtis Jones continues to progress from his recovery plan, with the academy graduate potentially available for selection in approximately three weeks, according to reports from the club's medical staff.

## Squad Rotation Plans

Slot has indicated his intention to rotate the squad strategically over the coming matches. With European and domestic competitions demanding consistency, the Liverpool manager emphasised the importance of managing player minutes carefully.

"We have an excellent squad at Liverpool," Slot stated during his press conference. "Players like Cody Gakpo, Luis Diaz, and Diogo Jota provide quality options across the forward line. The depth we possess allows us to maintain intensity and performance without overextending individuals."

The manager's approach reflects lessons learned throughout the season, with Liverpool's medical team working in tandem with coaching staff to ensure optimal performance whilst minimising injury risk.

## Goalkeeper Situation

Alisson Becker remains Liverpool's undisputed number one, with the Brazilian continuing to recover fully from previous concerns. Caoimhin Kelleher, the reliable backup option, is ready to feature should rotation become necessary.

## Looking Ahead

With the squad gradually approaching full availability, Slot appears confident Liverpool can maintain their competitive edge through this crucial period of the season. The manager's measured approach to player management suggests a club thinking strategically about long-term success rather than short-term gains.

Liverpool fans can expect to see their side at full strength soon, with Anfield continuing to serve as a fortress during this pivotal campaign.
