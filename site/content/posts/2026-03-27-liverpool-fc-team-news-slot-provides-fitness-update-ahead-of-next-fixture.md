# Liverpool FC Team News: Slot Provides Fitness Update Ahead of Next Fixture

Arne Slot has provided an encouraging injury update ahead of Liverpool FC's upcoming fixture, with several key players expected to return to full training in the coming week.

During his latest press conference at Anfield, the Reds manager confirmed that Virgil van Dijk has progressed well in his recovery programme and remains on track for a return sooner rather than later. The Dutch defender, crucial to Liverpool's defensive shape under Slot's stewardship, has been missed during his recent absence.

"Virgil is doing everything right in his rehabilitation," Slot explained. "We're being careful not to rush him back, but the signs are very positive. He's an integral part of what we're trying to build here at Liverpool."

Mohamed Salah, meanwhile, continues to train with the first team and faces no fresh concerns ahead of the weekend. The Egyptian winger remains a focal point of Liverpool's attacking play and is available for selection. Recent speculation regarding his future beyond the current campaign continues to circulate, though Slot has emphasised his importance to the club's present ambitions.

## Positive Progress Across the Squad

Luis Diaz has also stepped up his involvement in team training following a minor knock, whilst Dominik Szoboszlai maintains his regular routine without injury concerns. The Hungarian midfielder has been integral to Slot's midfield setup, alongside Ryan Gravenberch and Alexis Mac Allister, forming a competitive unit in Liverpool's engine room.

Defensively, Joe Gomez and Ibrahima Konate continue their partnerships at centre-back in van Dijk's absence, with both players delivering consistent performances. Jarell Quansah has also featured prominently this season, whilst Conor Bradley provides additional defensive cover on the right flank following Trent Alexander-Arnold's departure to Real Madrid last summer.

Alisson Becker remains fit and focused between the sticks, though backup goalkeeper Caoimhin Kelleher has provided solid backup when required.

## Squad Depth and Rotation

Slot has indicated that rotation may feature in upcoming fixtures as Liverpool balance their fixture congestion. Federico Chiesa continues to build match fitness following his summer arrival, whilst Cody Gakpo, Darwin Nunez, and Diogo Jota provide attacking options across the frontline.

In midfield, Curtis Jones has featured in recent matchdays, offering Slot tactical flexibility. Harvey Elliott also remains available, though his appearance schedule continues to be managed carefully.

Wataru Endo and Konstantinos Tsimikas provide further squad depth for the manager to utilise when tactical adjustments are required.

## Looking Ahead

With PSG's Ligue 1 fixture ahead of their Champions League encounter with Liverpool now postponed, the Reds have a slightly clearer picture regarding their schedule. Slot will use this window to ensure his squad is adequately prepared and as fit as possible for the intensity of competition ahead.

The manager stressed the importance of maintaining squad cohesion and fitness levels throughout the remainder of the 2025-26 season. "We have quality throughout this squad," Slot noted. "It's about keeping everyone sharp, managing workload, and ensuring we have a fully fit group when it matters most."

Liverpool supporters can expect updates on player availability as the club moves closer to their next fixture.
