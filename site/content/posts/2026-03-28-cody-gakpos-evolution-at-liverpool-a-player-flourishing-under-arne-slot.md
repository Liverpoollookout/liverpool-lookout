# Cody Gakpo's Evolution at Liverpool: A Player Flourishing Under Arne Slot

**Anfield, 28 March 2026** – When Cody Gakpo joined Liverpool from PSV Eindhoven in January 2023, few could have predicted the trajectory the Dutch winger would follow. Now, in the 2025-26 season under manager Arne Slot, Gakpo has become an indispensable attacking weapon, showcasing exactly why Liverpool's recruitment team identified him as a cornerstone piece of their future.

The transformation has been remarkable. No longer a promising talent waiting for consistency, Gakpo has evolved into one of the Premier League's most dangerous wide players, operating within Slot's meticulously constructed attacking framework alongside Mohamed Salah and Luis Diaz.

## Current Form and Recent Impact

Gakpo's contribution in Liverpool's dominant 4-0 Champions League victory over Galatasaray on 18 March perfectly encapsulates his current trajectory. The win at Anfield showcased not merely individual brilliance but Gakpo's complete integration into Liverpool's attacking philosophy – his positioning, intelligent movement, and clinical finishing all on display.

This season, Gakpo's numbers speak volumes. His combination of goals, assists, and chances created places him among Liverpool's top offensive contributors. More significantly, his consistency has rarely wavered across the campaign, a testament to both his professionalism and the tactical environment Slot has cultivated.

## Role Within Slot's System

Under Slot's guidance, Gakpo operates in a more refined system than his earlier Liverpool years. The manager, who previously worked in the Eredivisie with PSV, clearly understands how to maximise Gakpo's natural attributes. Playing predominantly on the left wing, Gakpo benefits from a defined role: press aggressively in possession, drift infield to create numerical advantages, and deliver dangerous crosses or cutbacks to Darwin Nunez and other advancing players.

The Dutch winger's intelligent movement has improved markedly. Rather than relying purely on pace and trickery, Gakpo now demonstrates sophisticated positional awareness – understand when to hold width, when to invert, and when to provide defensive support. This maturation reflects both coaching excellence and the player's own commitment to development.

His partnership with players like Ryan Gravenberch in midfield and Dominik Szoboszlai in creativity has proven particularly effective, with Gakpo serving as the final piece in Liverpool's attacking sequences.

## Strengths and Tactical Attributes

Gakpo possesses a rare combination of attributes that few wingers in world football can match:

**Versatility**: Operating across multiple attacking positions, Gakpo provides tactical flexibility. Whether deployed as a traditional winger or inverted onto his favoured right foot, he maintains effectiveness.

**Physical Attributes**: His pace remains genuine – not merely in straight-line speed but in explosive acceleration that creates separation from defenders. Combined with respectable strength, Gakpo can evade contact or hold possession effectively.

**Left Foot Quality**: The winger's technical ability on his natural left foot represents a critical asset. His crossing, passing accuracy, and finishing all demonstrate above-average technical standards.

**Pressing and Work Rate**: Slot's system demands relentless pressing, and Gakpo has embraced this requirement wholly. His defensive contribution, often overlooked, remains underrated.

## Areas for Continued Development

Whilst Gakpo's 2025-26 campaign has been genuinely impressive, refinement opportunities remain:

**Consistency in High-Pressure Moments**: Against the world's elite defences, Gakpo occasionally reverts to predictable patterns. Greater variation in his attacking play could enhance penetration.

**One-vs-One Finishing**: Whilst his overall conversion rate has improved, isolated finishing opportunities sometimes lack clinical edge compared to Salah's ruthlessness.

**Defensive Positioning**: Occasionally caught out positionally when defending transitions, Gakpo's reading of defensive situations could sharpen further.

## Statistical Context

The numbers provide objective assessment. Gakpo's goal-scoring return, combined with his assist tally and chance creation metrics, places him consistently in Liverpool's upper echelon of attacking contributors. His expected goals (xG) metrics suggest he's in the right positions; his conversion rates indicate improving clinical efficiency.

Possession recovery statistics and pressing success rates further illustrate his complete game development. These aren't merely attacking statistics – they reflect a modern winger who comprehends the 360-degree demands of contemporary football.

## The Fan Perspective

Anfield's support for Gakpo has grown substantially. What began as cautious optimism has evolved into genuine affection. The winger's commitment is visible; his willingness to improve transparent. Fans recognise that unlike some attacking players, Gakpo delivers both moments of exceptional skill and consistent, honest labour.

The departure of Trent Alexander-Arnold to Real Madrid and other changes to Liverpool's squad have paradoxically elevated Gakpo's importance. As the club builds around young, hungry talent – with established figures like Virgil van Dijk, Alisson Becker, and Salah remaining pillars – Gakpo represents the category of developed, proven performers upon whom sustained success is constructed.

## Looking Forward

As Liverpool pursue major honours, Cody Gakpo has positioned himself as integral to the project. Under Arne Slot's management, with the infrastructure and support systems clearly in place, Gakpo appears primed for career-defining seasons.

The Dutch international has moved beyond "exciting prospect" status. He is now a thoroughly professional, tactically aware attacking player operating at genuinely elite level. For Liverpool supporters, that represents not merely sporting value but confirmation that the club's strategic direction – targeting experienced, developable talent in their mid-twenties – yields dividends.
