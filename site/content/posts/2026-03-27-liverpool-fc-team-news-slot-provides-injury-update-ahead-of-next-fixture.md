# Liverpool FC Team News: Slot Provides Latest Injury Update

**Friday, 27 March 2026** – Arne Slot has provided an encouraging fitness update ahead of Liverpool FC's next fixture, with several key players expected to return to full training this week at Anfield.

The Liverpool manager addressed the squad's condition during his Friday press conference, confirming that the club's medical team has made significant progress with multiple players working their way back to availability.

## Key Players on the Mend

Mohamed Salah is progressing well in his recovery programme and could feature in the coming weeks, sources close to the club suggest. The Egyptian forward has been working with Liverpool's conditioning staff to regain full match fitness following a minor setback in recent weeks.

Virgil van Dijk, the club's captain and defensive cornerstone, continues his rehabilitation and is expected to return to team training early next week. The Dutch centre-back remains crucial to Slot's defensive plans at Anfield, and his availability will strengthen Liverpool's backline considerably.

Ryan Gravenberch and Alexis Mac Allister have both progressed positively, with both midfielders expected to feature in squad selection for the upcoming fixture. The midfield duo has been integral to Slot's tactical setup this season, and their return will add depth to Liverpool's engine room.

## Squad Rotation Expected

Slot indicated that squad rotation remains on the agenda, particularly given the demanding fixture schedule throughout the 2025-26 campaign. Joe Gomez, Ibrahima Konate, and Jarell Quansah are all competing for defensive berths, which should provide the manager with tactical flexibility.

Dominik Szoboszlai continues to impress in training and remains available for selection, whilst Harvey Elliott is working towards full integration following his recent appearances off the bench.

## Alisson and Caoimhin Kelleher

Alisson Becker, Liverpool's number one, is fit and ready for the week ahead. Backup goalkeeper Caoimhin Kelleher remains on standby should he be required, providing Slot with reliable depth between the sticks.

## Darwin Nunez and Attack

Darwin Nunez has trained regularly this week and is available for selection. The Uruguayan striker, alongside Luis Diaz and Cody Gakpo, forms part of Liverpool's attacking arsenal. Federico Chiesa continues to settle into life at Anfield following his arrival, and the Italian international is progressing well with the squad.

## No Fresh Concerns

Slot confirmed that there are no fresh injury concerns within the playing staff, though the manager will assess everyone in training sessions leading up to the next fixture. Konstantinos Tsimikas and Conor Bradley remain options at left-back and right-back respectively, with rotation likely across the defence.

Wataru Endo and Curtis Jones provide midfield depth, whilst Diogo Jota represents another attacking option should Slot rotate his forward line.

## Looking Ahead

The Liverpool manager has indicated that the club's medical department will continue to monitor all players closely, with particular emphasis on maintaining peak condition during this crucial period of the season. The return of key personnel should bolster Liverpool's challenge in the coming weeks.

Fans at Anfield can expect an energised squad with several senior figures back to full availability, strengthening Slot's selection options and tactical flexibility for the fixtures ahead.
