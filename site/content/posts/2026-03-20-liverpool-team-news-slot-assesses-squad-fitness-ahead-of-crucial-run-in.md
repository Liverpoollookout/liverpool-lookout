With the 2025-26 campaign entering its decisive phase, Arne Slot faces a careful balancing act as Liverpool navigate the final weeks of the season. The Reds' medical department has been working overtime to ensure key personnel are available for what promises to be a demanding stretch at Anfield and beyond.

## Injury Updates

The good news for Slot is that several influential figures are expected to be fit and available for selection. **Virgil van Dijk**, the defensive cornerstone, is understood to be in full training after managing a minor knock in recent weeks. The Dutch captain's presence remains paramount to Liverpool's title aspirations, and supporters will be relieved to see him on course for continued involvement.

**Mohamed Salah** has also trained without restriction and is expected to feature prominently in the coming fixtures. The Egyptian winger's form has been crucial to recent performances, and his availability strengthens Liverpool's attacking arsenal considerably.

**Darwin Núñez** continues to make positive progress in his recovery, with the striker reported to be increasing his involvement in first-team sessions. Whilst he may not be rushed back to full ninety-minute duties immediately, his return to the squad could provide Slot with additional attacking options.

## Notable Absences

However, Slot will need to manage his resources carefully. Several squad members remain on the sidelines, with their return dates subject to the club's cautious rehabilitation protocols. The manager has previously emphasised the importance of not forcing players back prematurely, particularly given the demanding fixture schedule.

**Harvey Elliott** is continuing his rehabilitation programme, though sources suggest his full return remains a few weeks away. The academy graduate's creative talents would be a welcome addition to the midfield once he's deemed fit.

## Suspension Watch

There are currently no reported suspension concerns affecting key personnel, though Slot will be acutely aware of accumulated cautions among senior players as the season progresses. Discipline remains essential in the run-in.

## Rotation Strategy

With fixtures coming thick and fast, rotation is expected to play a significant role in Slot's planning. The depth of his squad—featuring established stars like **Alexis Mac Allister**, **Luis Díaz**, **Dominik Szoboszlai**, and **Ryan Gravenberch**—should allow the manager to manage workloads without compromising competitive ambition.

The defence appears particularly well-stocked, with **Joe Gomez**, **Ibrahima Konaté**, **Jarell Quansah**, and **Trent Alexander-Arnold** providing options across the back line. Meanwhile, **Caoimhin Kelleher** continues to provide experienced backup to **Alisson Becker** in goal.

## Manager's Assessment

Slot has consistently stressed the importance of squad fitness and mental freshness during this critical period. His tactical flexibility—demonstrated by recent performances drawing praise for intensity and structure—suggests he's confident in rotating personnel without sacrificing Liverpool's competitive edge.

The coming weeks will test both his squad management and his tactical acumen. With every point now crucial, the manager's ability to keep his squad healthy and motivated could prove decisive in determining how this season concludes for the Merseyside club.
