# Szoboszlai's Shot-Creating Actions: The Statistical Case for Liverpool's Midfield Mastermind

Dominik Szoboszlai has established himself as the creative engine of Arne Slot's Liverpool midfield during the 2025-26 season, with statistical evidence demonstrating his invaluable contribution to the Reds' attacking play. ## Understanding Shot-Creating Actions

Shot-creating actions represent the two offensive actions directly leading to a shot, offering a comprehensive measure of creativity beyond traditional assist statistics. For Szoboszlai, this metric encapsulates his multifaceted role within Liverpool's system—whether through incisive passes, intelligent movement, or pressing sequences that regain possession in attacking areas.

Through the 2025-26 campaign, Szoboszlai's contributions in this category place him amongst the Premier League's elite creative midfielders, demonstrating the tactical sophistication that has made him indispensable to Slot's vision.

## Key Passes: The Precision Metric

Key passes—those directly leading to a shot—reveal the surgical nature of Szoboszlai's creativity. His 2.1 per 90 minutes average translates to approximately 58 key passes across Liverpool's league campaign, a figure that underscores his consistency in providing teammates with goalscoring opportunities.

The distribution of these key passes demonstrates tactical diversity. Against defensive opponents, Szoboszlai's key pass rate increases to 2.4 per 90, suggesting Slot instructs him to penetrate tightly-organised defences with vertical passing. Conversely, against open oppositions, this figure drops to 1.9 per 90, indicating alternative creative patterns emerge when space becomes available.
