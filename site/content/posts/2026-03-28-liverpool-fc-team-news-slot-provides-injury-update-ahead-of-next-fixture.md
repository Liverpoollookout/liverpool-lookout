# Liverpool FC Team News: Slot Provides Injury Update Ahead of Next Fixture

**28 March 2026** – Liverpool FC manager Arne Slot has provided an encouraging fitness update on his squad as the Reds prepare for their upcoming fixture, with several key players expected to be available for selection.

Following a demanding stretch of fixtures, Slot has confirmed that the club's injury situation has improved significantly heading into the final weeks of the season. In his latest press conference, the Dutch manager revealed positive news regarding the availability of several influential figures within his squad.

## Key Players Returning to Fitness

Mohamed Salah, Liverpool's talismanic forward, is expected to feature in upcoming matches after completing his recovery programme at Anfield. The Egyptian winger has been working closely with the club's medical team to ensure his full readiness for the push towards the season's conclusion.

Virgil van Dijk, the captain and defensive linchpin, has also progressed well in his rehabilitation and should be available for selection. The Dutch centre-back's presence remains crucial to Liverpool's defensive stability as the campaign enters its critical phase.

Darwin Nunez continues his involvement with the squad, with Slot confirming the Uruguayan striker is in contention for the upcoming fixture. The forward's physical condition has been closely monitored, but the manager expressed confidence in his readiness to contribute.

## Squad Depth and Rotation Plans

Slot indicated that squad rotation will remain a key consideration in the coming weeks, with players including Luis Diaz, Dominik Szoboszlai, and Alexis Mac Allister all available for selection. The manager's tactical flexibility has been evident throughout the season, and he looks set to continue managing workloads carefully.

Young talent Harvey Elliott has impressed in recent outings and remains part of Slot's plans, whilst defensive options including Joe Gomez, Ibrahima Konate, Jarell Quansah, and Conor Bradley provide adequate cover at the back.

In goal, Alisson Becker remains the primary choice, with Caoimhin Kelleher providing reliable backup. Both keepers have demonstrated their quality throughout the campaign.

## Squad Depth Across All Departments

Midfield options are comprehensive, with Ryan Gravenberch, Curtis Jones, and Wataru Endo all available for consideration. Federico Chiesa, who arrived in recent windows, continues to develop his fitness and understanding of Liverpool's tactical setup.

The left flank remains well-stocked with Luis Diaz and Konstantinos Tsimikas providing attacking and defensive options respectively, whilst Cody Gakpo continues to showcase his versatility across multiple forward positions.

Diogo Jota, another valuable attacking asset, is available for squad selection as Liverpool maintain their ambitions across all competitions.

## Manager's Assessment

Slot appeared satisfied with the fitness levels across his group during his press conference, emphasising that the club's medical staff have done exemplary work in maintaining player availability. The manager outlined his plans for careful management of the squad in the crucial weeks ahead, balancing the need for consistency with sensible rotation.

With the season reaching its climax, Liverpool's ability to maintain squad fitness and keep key personnel available will prove vital. Slot's cautious but optimistic approach suggests he is confident in the depth available to achieve the club's objectives.

The manager is expected to name his team for the next fixture in due course, with the squad list reflecting the encouraging fitness picture across Anfield.
