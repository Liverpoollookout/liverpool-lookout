# Liverpool FC Team News: Injury Updates and Squad Availability with Arne Slot

As Liverpool FC prepares for their next fixture, manager Arne Slot has provided an encouraging injury update on several key players, with the Reds looking to maintain momentum in what remains a crucial phase of the 2025-26 season.

## Key Players Assessed

During his latest press conference, Slot confirmed that captain **Virgil van Dijk** has resumed full training following a minor knock sustained in recent weeks. The Dutch centre-back is expected to be available for selection and remains integral to Liverpool's defensive setup at Anfield. Alongside Van Dijk, fellow defender **Ibrahima Konate** has also trained without restrictions, giving Slot a fully fit defensive pairing heading into the weekend.

**Mohamed Salah** continues his recovery from a minor muscular concern but is progressing well ahead of schedule. The Egyptian winger has been involved in modified training drills and is expected to be available for the upcoming match, though Slot has hinted at a cautious approach regarding potential rotation.

## Squad Rotation Plans

With an increasingly congested fixture schedule, Slot has indicated that rotation will play a key role in maintaining squad freshness. **Harvey Elliott** and **Curtis Jones** are both fit and vying for midfield opportunities, with the manager keen to utilise the depth available in his squad. Recent recruit **Federico Chiesa** has continued his rehabilitation and is edging closer to full competitive involvement.

**Ryan Gravenberch** and **Alexis Mac Allister** remain central to Slot's midfield plans, with both players showing no fitness concerns. The manager praised their consistent availability throughout the campaign.

## Forward Options

**Darwin Nunez** and **Luis Diaz** are both fully available, providing Liverpool with attacking potency across the frontline. **Cody Gakpo** has trained normally this week and remains a viable option for the weekend's fixture. **Diogo Jota** continues his recovery programme and is expected to return to first-team training imminently.

In goal, **Alisson Becker** continues as the undisputed number one, with **Caoimhin Kelleher** providing backup cover. Both 'keepers have maintained excellent fitness throughout the season.

## Defensive Depth

At full-back, **Joe Gomez** has recovered well from recent fitness work and is available for selection. **Konstantinos Tsimikas** provides experienced cover on the left flank, whilst **Conor Bradley** continues to develop his game and remains in Slot's squad plans. The loss of **Trent Alexander-Arnold** to Real Madrid last summer has necessitated the reshuffle of Liverpool's full-back options, but Slot appears satisfied with the solutions available.

## Suspended Players

No Liverpool players are currently serving suspensions, meaning Slot has the full complement of his first-team squad available for selection heading into this period.

## Looking Ahead

Slot's squad management has been notably efficient this season, with the manager balancing the demands of domestic and European competition effectively. The return of key personnel to full fitness strengthens Liverpool's prospects significantly. With **Van Dijk**, **Salah**, and the supporting cast all available or close to availability, the Reds appear well-positioned for their upcoming challenge.

The manager has emphasised the importance of maintaining intensity during this phase of the season, with every fixture carrying significant implications for Liverpool's final standings.
