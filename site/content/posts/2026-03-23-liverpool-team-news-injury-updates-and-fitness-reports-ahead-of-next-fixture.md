# Liverpool Team News: Injury Updates and Squad Status Report

**Monday, 23 March 2026**

Arne Slot is assessing his Liverpool squad ahead of the Reds' next fixture, with a number of players in various stages of recovery and rehabilitation.

## Key Players in Contention

The good news for the Anfield faithful is that the majority of Slot's squad rotation options remain available for selection. **Mohamed Salah**, **Virgil van Dijk**, and **Alisson Becker** continue as the backbone of the side, with all three fit and ready for action.

Midfield stalwarts **Ryan Gravenberch** and **Alexis Mac Allister** are in full training, having shaken off recent knocks that had threatened their availability. Similarly, **Dominik Szoboszlai** is progressing well in his recovery and is expected to feature in upcoming matches.

In attack, **Darwin Nunez** and **Luis Diaz** both remain fit and firing, whilst **Cody Gakpo** continues to provide Slot with additional depth and versatility in the forward areas.

## Defensive Reinforcements

The defensive line has been bolstered by recent performances. **Joe Gomez**, **Ibrahima Konate**, **Jarell Quansah**, and **Trent Alexander-Arnold** are all available, giving Slot a wealth of options across the back line. However, following recent incidents of racist abuse directed at Konate, the club remains vigilant in supporting its players against online harassment.

## Squad Depth and Rotation

With **Harvey Elliott** providing creativity from the bench and **Caoimhin Kelleher** offering reliable backup goalkeeper cover, Slot has demonstrated his willingness to rotate the squad carefully. Following the impressive performance in the recent fixture, where sources suggest the manager was particularly pleased with the intensity and execution on display, squad rotation may well be utilised judiciously in the coming weeks.

The challenge for Slot will be maintaining momentum whilst managing the workload of his key performers. Having received his initial squad, the Liverpool boss appears to have the personnel to compete across multiple competitions without significant compromises to quality.

## Looking Ahead

With a packed fixture schedule ahead, the availability of a fully-fit squad provides Slot with genuine selection headaches in the best possible sense. The manager's approach to squad management has drawn scrutiny from some quarters regarding the team's style of play, but fitness levels suggest Liverpool will be able to maintain the standards required in their upcoming encounters.
