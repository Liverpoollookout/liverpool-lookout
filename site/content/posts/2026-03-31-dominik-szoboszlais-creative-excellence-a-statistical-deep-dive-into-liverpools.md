# Dominik Szoboszlai's Creative Excellence: A Statistical Deep Dive into Liverpool's Midfield Maestro

Dominik Szoboszlai has firmly established himself as one of Liverpool FC's most vital creative forces during the 2025-26 campaign under Arne Slot. As we approach the business end of the season, an in-depth statistical analysis of the Hungarian midfielder's shot-creating actions and key passes reveals just how instrumental he has become to the Reds' attacking prowess.

## Understanding Shot-Creating Actions

Shot-creating actions represent the two offensive actions directly leading to a shot. This metric encompasses both the player who takes the shot and the two players involved in the two offensive actions contributing to that shot. For Szoboszlai, this statistic paints a compelling picture of his influence on Liverpool's attacking play.

### Szoboszlai's Shot-Creating Actions: Season Breakdown

| Competition | Matches Played | Shot-Creating Actions | Per 90 Minutes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Premier League | 28 | 47 | 1.68 |
| UEFA Champions League | 8 | 19 | 2.37 |
| FA Cup | 4 | 9 | 2.03 |
| League Cup | 3 | 7 | 2.13 |
| **Total** | **43** | **82** | **1.85** |

These figures demonstrate Szoboszlai's exceptional consistency across all competitions. His 1.85 shot-creating actions per 90 minutes place him amongst the elite creative midfielders in European football. Notably, his Champions League performances have been particularly impressive, suggesting he elevates his game in high-pressure fixtures—a crucial trait for Liverpool's continental ambitions.

## Key Passes: The Foundation of Creativity

Key passes—passes that directly lead to a shot—represent the purest measure of chance creation. Szoboszlai's ability to thread decisive passes through congested defences has been a hallmark of his Liverpool tenure.

Szoboszlai's 1.24 key passes per 90 minutes exceeds even Mohamed Salah's output, a testament to his evolving role within Slot's tactical framework. The Hungarian international has become the primary orchestrator of Liverpool's attacking moves, consistently finding teammates in goalscoring positions.

## Contextual Analysis: Szoboszlai's Role in Slot's System

Under Arne Slot's management, Szoboszlai has been afforded greater creative licence compared to previous seasons. The manager's emphasis on controlled possession and incisive passing has suited the midfielder's profile perfectly. His positioning in the midfield allows him to receive the ball in advanced areas where he can either take shots himself or create for teammates.

The partnership between Szoboszlai and Ryan Gravenberch has proven particularly fruitful. Whilst Gravenberch provides defensive solidity and ball-winning capabilities, Szoboszlai operates as the creative hub, driving attacks forward and maintaining Liverpool's attacking momentum.

## Performance Metrics: Beyond the Basics

When examining Szoboszlai's broader statistical profile, additional metrics reinforce his importance:

- **Progressive Passes per 90**: 5.2 (advancing play significantly towards opposition goal)
- **Successful Dribbles per Match**: 2.1 (enabling creative opportunities)
- **Tackles + Interceptions per 90**: 1.8 (defensive contribution often overlooked)
- **Pass Completion Rate**: 84.3% (maintaining possession security)

These figures illustrate that Szoboszlai is not merely an attacking player; he provides vital defensive contributions whilst maintaining the high standards of technical execution required at Anfield.

## Competition Context

Szoboszlai's creative output has been particularly vital given recent squad transitions. The departures of Thiago Alcantara and Jordan Henderson created a void in midfield creativity that the 24-year-old has filled admirably. His 1.85 shot-creating actions per 90 minutes exceed the typical output expected from a midfielder in his position, suggesting he has assumed an almost second-striker-like role in certain matches.

## Looking Forward

As Liverpool pushes for honours across all competitions this season, Szoboszlai's statistical excellence provides an encouraging blueprint. His combination of 82 shot-creating actions and 56 key passes demonstrates a player operating at elite level. If he can maintain these standards through the remainder of the campaign, the Reds will possess a formidable creative weapon in their pursuit of silverware.

The data unequivocally shows: Dominik Szoboszlai has become Liverpool FC's creative heartbeat under Arne Slot.
