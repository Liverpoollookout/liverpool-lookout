# Liverpool FC Team News: Arne Slot Provides Squad Fitness Update Ahead of Next Fixture

**29 March 2026** - Arne Slot has delivered a positive injury update ahead of Liverpool FC's next fixture, with several key players expected to return to full availability after recent fitness concerns.

The Liverpool manager addressed the media during his latest press conference at Anfield, providing encouraging news on the status of Mohamed Salah, Virgil van Dijk, and Darwin Nunez, who have been monitored closely in recent weeks.

## Salah and Van Dijk Recovery on Track

Mohamed Salah is progressing well in his recovery programme and remains on schedule for the upcoming fixture. The Egyptian winger, who has been integral to Liverpool's attacking play throughout the 2025-26 season, is expected to return to full training this week pending final assessment.

Defence leader Virgil van Dijk has also responded positively to treatment, with Slot confirming the Dutch centre-back should be available for selection. Van Dijk's presence remains crucial to Liverpool's defensive stability at Anfield, and his return will bolster the backline alongside Ibrahima Konate and Joe Gomez.

## Darwin Nunez Progressing Well

Strike force Darwin Nunez is similarly progressing through his recovery protocol. The Uruguayan forward has been working with Liverpool's medical team to ensure his safe return to competitive action. Slot hinted that squad rotation may be employed in upcoming matches to manage workload across the forward line, which also includes Luis Diaz and Cody Gakpo.

## Midfield Options Available

In midfield, Ryan Gravenberch and Alexis Mac Allister remain fit and available for selection. Dominik Szoboszlai continues his rehabilitation programme with expected availability confirmed for upcoming fixtures. Curtis Jones and Harvey Elliott provide additional depth in the engine room, with both players fit and ready for action.

## Squad Rotation Planning

Slot has indicated that strategic squad rotation will be employed in the coming weeks, particularly across the forward and midfield positions. This approach aims to manage the physical demands on Liverpool's key players whilst maintaining consistency and performance levels.

Federico Chiesa, who joined the Reds from Juventus, continues his integration into the squad with regular training participation. The Italian winger offers additional attacking flexibility as Liverpool juggle fixture congestion.

## Defensive Depth

In defence, Liverpool has adequate cover with Joe Gomez, Ibrahima Konate, and Jarell Quansah all available. Konstantinos Tsimikas provides left-back options, whilst Conor Bradley offers competition on the right flank following Trent Alexander-Arnold's departure to Real Madrid in summer 2024.

Caoimhin Kelleher remains backup goalkeeper to Alisson Becker, with the Brazilian shot-stopper fit and available for selection.

## Looking Ahead

With suspended players returning to availability and injuries managed effectively, Slot has confirmed that Liverpool will field a competitive squad for the next fixture. The manager's focus remains on maintaining fitness levels whilst building momentum during the crucial run-in of the season.

Liverpool supporters can expect to see familiar faces return to action, with Slot's management of player welfare suggesting the club is planning for both immediate fixtures and the remainder of the campaign.

**Further updates will be provided following the next training session at Anfield.**
